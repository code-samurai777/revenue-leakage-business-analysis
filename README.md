# revenue-leakage-business-analysis
End-to-end business analytics case study identifying revenue leakage and growth opportunities using SQL and Python.

# Revenue Leakage & Growth Strategy Analysis

## Business Problem
A growing digital business is experiencing stagnant revenue despite increasing customer acquisition.
This project investigates where revenue is being lost and identifies actionable strategies to improve profitability.

## Objectives
- Identify revenue leakage across the customer lifecycle
- Analyze customer retention, churn, and discount effectiveness
- Recommend data-backed business actions to improve revenue and margins

## Dataset
Transactional and customer-level data including:
- Customers
- Orders
- Payments
- Discounts and refunds
(Time period: TBD)

## Tools & Skills
- SQL: joins, CTEs, window functions
- Python: pandas, numpy, matplotlib
- Analytics: cohort analysis, funnel analysis, LTV
- Business metrics: revenue, churn, retention, AOV

## Key Deliverables
- SQL scripts for core analysis
- Python notebooks for deep-dive analysis
- Executive-level summary with recommendations
